1

Create Project

2

Add Experiments

Define your experimental steps with the four-panel framework. Link literature. Set benchmarks.

3

Upload & Analyze

Drop your CSV. Pick a skill. AI processes the data and returns publication-quality charts with statistical summaries.

4

Present & Export

Present your work as a slide deck. Export charts and tables for your paper, report, or grant proposal.
